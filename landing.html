answers and questions

What is Laqfoil Stretch Ceilings ?

Laqfoil Stretch Ceilings is a European concept of finishing new and existing surfaces, by use of a stretchable membrane. Stretch Ceiling Technology offers products that are alternative to drywall or drop tiles. It completely eliminates need of use of a drywall or any other similar product for a new developments. Laqfoil Stretch Ceiling applications could be used to enhance an existing or new finishes. Upon installation it provides perfectly leveled, ideally flat and appealing finishing.

Why Laqfoil Stretch Ceilings better then regular drywall or drop ceilings ?

Stretch ceilings are very beneficial in a variety of ways. Not only do these hide all kinds of unsightly features present on your ceilings and/or walls but also tend to create an everlasting, aesthetically appealing impression on everyone. Just some of the benefits of Laqfoil Stertch Ceilings are; Aesthetical Appeal, Versatility, Practicality, Safe and Durable, Eco-Friendly, Fast and clean installation, etc

Where could Laqfoil Stretch Ceilings be used ?

Laqfoil Stretch Ceilings could be used in any place. Whether it’s a residential or commercial, public place or corporate office, medical facility or entertainment establishment, our product would enhance your place, making it practical, attractive, aesthetically appealing and unique.
